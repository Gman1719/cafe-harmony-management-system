Why Choose Markan

Home of Authentic Ethiopian Spices

Our dishes are crafted with traditional berbere, mitmita, and authentic spice blends passed down through generations.

Locally-Sourced Ingredients

We partner with local farmers in the Amhara Region to bring you the freshest produce and highest quality ingredients.

Ethiopia's Timeless Coffee Ritual

Experience the traditional coffee ceremony, a UNESCO-recognized cultural heritage, prepared with 100% organic Ethiopian beans.

Family-Friendly Atmosphere

Warm hospitality, comfortable seating, and a welcoming environment for students, faculty, and families alike.
