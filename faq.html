We're located on the main campus of Debre Birhan University, near the library. Look for our traditional Ethiopian coffee ceremony sign!

Yes, there is parking available for university staff and visitors near our location. Street parking is also available nearby.

Absolutely! Ethiopian cuisine has many delicious vegan and vegetarian options. Try our Shiro Wat, Misir Wat, Gomen, or the vegetarian combination platter. All our vegetarian dishes are clearly marked on the menu.

Injera is a sourdough flatbread made from teff flour, which is naturally gluten-free. It's the staple of Ethiopian cuisine and used to scoop up stews and dishes. We offer both traditional teff injera and mixed injera.

Ethiopian food can range from mild to very spicy. Many dishes use berbere spice which has some heat, but we can adjust spice levels to your preference. Just let your server know how spicy you'd like your meal!

Yes! In addition to gluten-free teff injera, many of our stews and dishes are naturally gluten-free. Please inform our staff about your dietary needs, and we'll guide you to suitable options.

The Ethiopian coffee ceremony is a traditional ritual where green coffee beans are roasted, ground, and brewed in a clay pot called a jebena. It's served in three rounds and accompanied by popcorn or traditional snacks. It's a symbol of hospitality and community.

While walk-ins are always welcome, we recommend making a reservation, especially for weekend evenings and for groups larger than 4 people. You can book through our website, by phone, or in person.

You can make a reservation by clicking the "Reserve Now" button on our homepage, calling us at +251 906 902 551, or visiting us in person. Online reservations are confirmed immediately via email.

Yes, we accommodate groups up to 20 people. For parties larger than 8, please call us directly at least 48 hours in advance so we can prepare the best seating arrangement for you.

We kindly ask that you cancel at least 2 hours before your reservation time. For groups larger than 6, please give us 24 hours notice. You can cancel by calling us or through the confirmation email.

Yes, we deliver within the Debre Birhan University campus and nearby areas. Delivery is available during our opening hours. Minimum order for delivery is 150 ETB.

Delivery within the university campus is free for orders over 150 ETB. For nearby neighborhoods, there's a small delivery fee of 30-50 ETB depending on distance.

Delivery typically takes 30-45 minutes within the campus area, and 45-60 minutes for nearby neighborhoods. During peak hours, it may take a little longer.
