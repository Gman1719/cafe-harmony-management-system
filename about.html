Our Story

Founded in 2020 by a group of passionate culinary artists from Debre Birhan University, Markan Cafe started with a simple vision: to share the rich flavors of Ethiopian cuisine with students, faculty, and the local community. What began as a small coffee stand has grown into a beloved gathering place known for authentic doro wat, traditional coffee ceremonies, and warm hospitality.
